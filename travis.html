About the Event

Travis Scott will be performing in India for the first time as part of his Circus Maximus World Tour, with concerts scheduled for October 18 and 19, 2025, at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in New Delhi. The initial concert was scheduled for October 18th, but due to high demand, an additional show on October 19th was announced.

Experience the electrifying performance of one of the biggest names in hip-hop and rap, known for his high-energy shows and chart-topping hits like "SICKO MODE", "goosebumps", and "BUTTERFLY EFFECT".
